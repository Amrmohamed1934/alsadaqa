Who Are We?

AL SADAQA is a multidisciplinary company delivering state-of-the-art projects and solutions across high-performance sectors including electro-mechanical, low current, information technology, telecommunication and network security sectors.

AL SADAQA is operating in the Saudi Arabia since 1995 and al sadaqa has achieved tremendous growth as one of the leading companies in the region providing a wide spectrum of leading edge as well as niche products.

Through our operations, we provide integrated design, functionality, and efficiency from beginning to end.

Our group of engineers are specialized in design, sales, installation, maintenance & project management of all areas in our fields.
